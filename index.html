Intelligence Highlights

Key findings for leadership review

Nokia Bell Labs (Murray Hill) is NJ's #1 photonics employer — confirmed DMTS/EVP-level researchers across PIC, coherent optics, and optical access research.
Coherent Corp. (Budd Lake/Watchung) is rapidly expanding — Deputy CTO, VP-level, and Director-level NJ talent confirmed; 18 open NJ postings, highest active velocity.
Acacia / Cisco (Plainsboro/Holmdel) holds 5+ NJ silicon photonics + DSP engineers post-acquisition; strong pipeline indicator.
NJ Startup Cluster emerging — Quantum Transistors, AQRONOS, Aloe Semiconductor (Nubis (now integrated into Ciena after Sept 2025 acquisition)) forming a PIC/silicon photonics corridor in Monmouth/Somerset — with Ciena gaining 50+ NJ engineers via the $270M Nubis acquisition (Sep 2025).
Critical Supply Gap: Senior PIC Design Engineers (8+ YOE) are <8% of NJ supply. Undersea/EDFA specialists (SubCom, OFS) are near-zero replaceable from within NJ alone.

Key NJ Photonics Corridors

Major employment clusters and their anchor companies

Monmouth Corridor (Red Bank · Holmdel · Middletown): Nokia Bell Labs, Acacia/Cisco, SubCom, MACOM — 68 professionals, largest concentration. Anchor: telecom/coherent optical.
Middlesex Hub (Plainsboro · Piscataway · Cranbury · Fords): Acacia, HORIBA, TRUMPF Photonics, Princeton spinouts — 52 professionals. Anchor: specialty optics + photonic devices.
Morris Valley (Murray Hill · Morristown · Florham Park): Nokia Bell Labs HQ, CACI/LGS Labs, General Dynamics — 47 professionals. Anchor: research + defense photonics.
Somerset Triangle (Warren · Watchung · Bridgewater): Coherent Corp. Budd Lake, OFS Fitel, Nubis talent (integrated into Ciena, Sept 2025), AQRONOS — 38 professionals. Anchor: EDFA + transceiver + PIC startup ecosystem.
Mercer / Princeton (Princeton · Princeton Junction): NEC Labs, SubCom, Quantum Computing Inc., university spinouts — 29 professionals. Anchor: R&D + quantum photonics.
